1. Flight Planning & Operations
Route optimization - Calculate shortest paths between any airports considering:

Aircraft range limitations

No-fly zones (restricted airspace, conflict zones)

Weather patterns (real-time integration)

Alternate airport suggestions - During emergencies, find nearest suitable airports based on aircraft type and runway requirements

Fuel planning - Distance-based fuel calculations with real-time pricing data

2. Safety & Emergency Response
Search and Rescue (SAR) - Identify all airports/hospitals within search radius

Emergency landing sites - Map all potential landing zones (not just airports - highways, clearings) based on terrain analysis

Incident command center - Show nearest fire stations, medical facilities, and their response capabilities

3. Infrastructure Planning (For KCAA/KAA)
Capacity analysis - Visualize airport congestion, predict future needs

Maintenance scheduling - Track runway conditions, lighting systems, nav-aids status

Development impact assessment - Show how new airports would affect existing traffic patterns

Budget allocation - Visualize which airports need urgent upgrades vs maintenance

4. Economic & Tourism Development
Tourism corridor mapping - Connect airports to national parks, resorts, attractions

Cargo/logistics optimization - Show freight routes, cold chain facilities, cargo capacity

Regional economic impact - Heat maps of airport influence on local economies

5. Regulatory & Compliance (KCAA specific)
Airspace management - Visualize controlled zones, restricted areas

Compliance monitoring - Track noise pollution, emissions near populated areas

Permit processing - Interactive tool for aviation license applications showing:

Proximity to existing infrastructure

Environmental impact zones

Community distance requirements

6. Pilot Training & Education
Virtual navigation - Practice flight planning with real constraints

Emergency procedure training - Simulate engine failure, find nearest suitable landing

Airport familiarization - Detailed airport layouts, approach patterns

7. Weather Integration
Real-time weather overlays - Wind patterns, visibility, cloud cover

Predictive analytics - Suggest optimal departure times based on forecasts

Divert alert - Auto-notify when airports fall below minimum weather requirements

8. Disaster Management
Natural disaster response - Identify operational airports after earthquakes/floods

Humanitarian aid routing - Optimize relief supply distribution

Evacuation planning - Show evacuation routes to multiple airports simultaneously
